Privacy Policy for QuickScan - QR & Barcode

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Introduction

QuickScan ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our QR code and barcode scanning application handles your information.

Information Collection and Use

Camera Permission

QuickScan requires camera access to scan QR codes and barcodes. The camera is used exclusively for:

  • Real-time scanning of QR codes and barcodes
  • Local image processing to decode scanned content

Important: All camera processing happens locally on your device. We do NOT:

  • Upload or transmit camera images to any server
  • Store camera images permanently
  • Share camera data with third parties
  • Use camera data for any purpose other than scanning

Data Storage

QuickScan stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Scan history (scanned QR codes and barcodes)
  • Downloaded files (when you choose to download content)
  • App preferences and settings

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Third-Party Services

QuickScan does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage data stays private and local to your device.

Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to your data.

Children's Privacy

QuickScan does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app can be safely used by users of all ages as no data is collected or transmitted.

Permissions Used

  • CAMERA: Required for scanning QR codes and barcodes. Images are processed in real-time and not stored.
  • INTERNET: Used only for processing web URLs found in scanned codes (when you choose to open them).
  • STORAGE: Used only when you choose to download files from scanned codes.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear scan history anytime from the app
  • Delete downloaded files from your device
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
